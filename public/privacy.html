Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026 · vinylbattle.com

Who we are

Vinyl Battle (vinylbattle.com) is a free browser game about record shops. No sign-up or login is required.

What data we collect

We do not collect personally identifiable data. The game stores a few preferences only in your browser (local storage); they never leave your device in an identifiable way:

  • your shop name;
  • your chosen language (Portuguese or English);
  • your solo-mode high score.

You can delete this data anytime by clearing the site data in your browser. Multiplayer games live temporarily in the server's memory and are discarded when the room empties.

Cookies and ads

We show ads through Google AdSense. Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to this and other websites.

  • Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to users based on their visit to our site and/or other sites on the Internet.
  • You may opt out of personalized advertising in your Google Ads Settings.
  • Learn how Google uses data at policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites, and see Google's advertising policies.
  • For visitors from the European Union and the United Kingdom, we display a consent notice before using advertising cookies.

Third-party data (album catalog)

Album information comes from MusicBrainz and ListenBrainz, and cover art from the Cover Art Archive, used for informational purposes. All trademarks, album names, artists and cover art belong to their respective rights holders.

Children

The game is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from children.

Your rights

Since we do not collect personally identifiable data on our servers, there is no account to delete on our side. Local data stays under your control in the browser. Privacy questions can be sent to the contact below.

Changes
